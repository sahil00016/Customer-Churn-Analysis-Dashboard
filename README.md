# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior using a telecom dataset to identify key factors influencing customer retention and attrition. The dashboard transforms raw customer data into actionable insights for business decision-making.

The objective was to understand why customers leave and identify high-risk segments so companies can implement retention strategies.

---

## Business Problem
Customer churn directly impacts revenue and growth. Companies need to identify patterns behind churn to reduce customer loss and improve retention strategies.

---

## Dataset Information
- Domain: Telecom Industry
- Records: Customer-level data
- Features include:
  - Contract Type
  - Monthly Charges
  - Tenure
  - Internet Service
  - Support Services
  - Churn Status

---

## Analytical Goals
This analysis answers key business questions:

- Which customers are most likely to churn?
- Does tenure affect churn probability?
- Which service plans have highest churn?
- Do higher charges increase churn risk?
- What customer segments require retention focus?

---

## Dashboard Features
The Power BI dashboard includes:

- KPI cards for customer metrics
- Contract-wise churn analysis
- Tenure vs churn trend
- Internet service churn comparison
- Pricing vs churn scatter analysis
- Interactive filters for segmentation

---

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts show highest churn rate
- Customers with short tenure churn most frequently
- Higher monthly charges correlate with churn risk
- Certain internet services have significantly higher churn rates

---

## Business Recommendations
Based on findings:

- Offer incentives for long-term contracts
- Target new customers with onboarding engagement
- Review pricing strategy for high-cost plans
- Provide retention offers for high-risk segments
